## 🚀 About me

- 🧑‍💻 Full-Stack developer — **React, Next.js & TypeScript** on the front end, **Node.js, Strapi & Laravel** on the back.
- 🏢 Currently building enterprise logistics products: [**Consolid**](https://consolid.ai/) (freight management) and [**Wareflow**](https://www.wareflow.ai/en/) (warehouse management), plus their companion mobile apps.
- 🛍️ Shipped [**maryline.ua**](https://maryline.ua) — a production **Next.js 16 + Strapi 5** fashion e-commerce store.
- 🎯 I care about clean architecture, thoughtful UI/UX, performance and accessibility.
